## Why This Matters Now

RLVR is now part of the training recipe for **all major language models**.

### The open science milestone

**Tulu 3** (Allen AI, November 2024):

- First fully open RLVR paper &mdash; released data, code, models, and full training recipe
- Built on Llama 3.1 (8B, 70B, 405B); competitive with GPT-4o-mini and Claude 3.5 Haiku

### The broader landscape

- **DeepSeek-R1** (Jan 2025): RLVR at massive scale with emergent chain-of-thought (caveats: distilled from US models, state-backed censorship)
- RLVR principles now appear across the industry

### The trend

For tasks where ground truth exists, **verifiable rewards are replacing human preferences**. The question: *for which tasks can we build verifiers?*
