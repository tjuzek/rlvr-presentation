## The Human Side

### RLVR shifts the labor frontier &mdash; but doesn't eliminate human involvement

Even with RLVR, someone still **curates problems**, **writes verifiers**, and **builds benchmarks**. For everything in the "subjective" half of the spectrum, **human judgment remains irreplaceable**.

### The deeper connection

RLVR reduces dependence on human preference data for some tasks &mdash; but the preference data industry is *growing*, because the remaining tasks are harder and require more expertise. The workers producing this data are often the same professionals whose careers AI is disrupting.

Platforms such as **Mercor**, **Scale AI**, and **Surge AI** now hire lawyers, scientists, and writers as gig workers to produce this expert data &mdash; what *NY Magazine* called *"the largest harvesting of human expertise ever attempted."*

**Lan will now show us what that work actually looks like.**
