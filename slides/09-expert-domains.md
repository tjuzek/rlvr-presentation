## From Domain Experts to Universal Expertise

#### The idea *(conceptual illustration)*

Domain experts contribute problems with verified answers in their fields:

<div id="chart-expert-domains" class="chart-container" style="height: 440px;"></div>

RLVR combines expert knowledge across domains &mdash; producing a model that doesn't just average performance, but **excels in each**.

<small>Illustration is conceptual. Tulu 3 demonstrated this on math and instruction following; the principle extends to any domain with verifiable answers.</small>

<aside class="notes">
Click through the animation: first you see individual expert groups, each strong in their domain. Then RLVR training combines all of them, and the model achieves high performance across the board. This is aspirational but grounded — Tulu 3 showed transfer even to tasks not in the RLVR training set.
</aside>
